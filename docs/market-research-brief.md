## The product

2020EV is a SaaS app for condo / apartment buildings that share EV charger(s).
It runs on top of a building's EXISTING networked Level 2 charger (ChargePoint
today) — there is NO hardware to sell or install. It provides: fair scheduling
(priority days + live charger status), automatic per-session electricity billing
to each resident's prepaid wallet at the building's real rate, a community feed,
and an admin dashboard for the building manager. It's live and multi-tenant.

Pricing: $199 / building / month flat ($99/mo founding rate for early reference
customers). No per-kWh markup, no transaction fees, no hardware. So ARPA ≈ $2,388
/building/year ($1,188 at founding rate).

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

- Residential CONDO / HOA buildings (and smaller multifamily / apartment
  communities) that ALREADY have one or a few shared, networked Level 2 chargers
  in a common garage or lot.
- Roughly 10–80 units, where residents share 1–3 chargers (so scheduling +
  cost-recovery is a real, recurring headache).
- The building/HOA currently either eats the electricity cost or tracks it
  manually (spreadsheets, honor system) — that's the pain we remove.
- Geography (in priority order): Miami-Dade County first; then Broward and Palm
  Beach (greater Miami / South Florida).

Disqualifiers (exclude these): buildings with NO EV charger; single-family homes;
new luxury towers where every space has its own dedicated, separately-metered
charger; buildings already using a full paid-charging network billing product
that's working for them.
